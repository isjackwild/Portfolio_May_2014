Joha Childmode

While working with Bacon de Czar, I got the chance to work on the new website for the children's clothing brand, Joha.

My roles in the development of the site, were the menu hover-state animations, made in CSS, and the development of the websites' 'ChildMode', where I worked with Backbone, Box2DWeb and a Sprite Animation Library.

Childmode

Childmode is a layer on top of the Joha site, which uses a Box2D Physics Engine and Sprite Sheet Animations to create an interactive world where Joha's sheep mascots perform all kinds of tricks. There are also other objects to play with, including a garden with generative flowers, starbursts, and my personal favourite: an Easter-Egg you can't knock over.

Visit the Joha site and click the top-right button to try it out for yourself.
