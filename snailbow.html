Snailbow Children's Story

Snailbow is an interactive childrenâ€™s story I made as a personal project. Having seen the rising popularity of paralax-scroll websites over the last couple of years, I wanted to try a new take on the technique, and use it to make an animated story. This project was also the perfect opportunity for me to explore character design and new visual aesthetics.

Meet Snailbow.

STORY AND CHARACTER DESIGN

I started by coming up with the character concept and story to tell. I developed a short story in which Snailbow goes on a quest to find a new home, meeting many of his friends along the way.

Took me a few goes to get the eyes right!

Hand Type

I wanted to try my hand at some hand-type, and this was the perfect project to do it. It took a lot of trial and error, but I think it paid off.

BRINGING SNAILBOW TO LIFE

After I had finished the storyboard and all the illustrations, I coded the website. The animation is achieved using Javascript to control the CSS of the various elements.
